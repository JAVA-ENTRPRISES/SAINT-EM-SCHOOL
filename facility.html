Campus tour

Facilities

Rooms and zones are planned around how children actually move, listen, and concentrate—not copied from a generic brochure layout.

What you will notice on a walk-through

Each block below maps to a physical space you can visit during an open day.

Smart classrooms

Bright rooms with boards, projectors where needed, and seating plans that allow both group work and focused individual tasks.

Science laboratories

Physics, chemistry, and biology labs with standard apparatus, safety signage, and teacher demonstration zones.

Computer laboratory

Structured keyboarding, coding introductions, and safe browsing habits—devices maintained on a rotation schedule.

Library & reading loft

Fiction and non-fiction stacks, reference sets, and a quieter mezzanine for older students preparing presentations.

Sports & movement

Outdoor ground for track and field, shaded courts, and indoor options for monsoon months.

Auditorium

Weekly assemblies, guest lectures, and annual productions with controlled lighting and clear sight-lines.

Art & craft studio

Mess-friendly surfaces, drying racks, and storage for ceramics, paints, and display boards.

Music & movement room

Acoustic treatment, instruments on loan, and mirrored wall for dance and rhythm work.

Wellness corner

First-aid readiness, rest bed, and coordination with local clinics for emergencies.
